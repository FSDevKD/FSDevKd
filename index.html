Why?

As a child of the 90s, I have witnessed firsthand the rapid evolution of technology throughout my lifetime. Growing up with Nintendo 64s and Sony PlayStations or Nokia phones, I have been captivated by the progression of gaming and other technological advancements. Growing up in Atlanta, Ga life was different. I had the opportunity to work for countless music artists because I was an assistant & social media manager at a record label for a road manager. I have been deemed the X factor because I was young and current. helped cultivate amazing branding rollouts and online presences for artists such as Kcamp, Syari da Kid, Sahbabii, and Dj Knockout. And more. Garnishing millions of views across many different platforms and when at events and concerts doing marketing we helped develop clients' lifelong fans. As I embark on the next chapter of my life as a software engineer, I am excited to apply my passion for technology to help create stunning brand websites for your company. With a keen eye for aesthetics and a deep understanding of the latest technological trends, I am confident in my ability to bring your company's vision to life. My goal is to not only meet but exceed your expectations and set the bar for what a successful online brand should be. With my expertise and dedication, I am committed to delivering exceptional results that will help your company thrive in today's fast-paced, technologically advanced world.
